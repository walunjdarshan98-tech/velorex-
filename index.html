The Story Behind Every Watch

Craftsmanship

Our master watchmakers collaborate with material scientists to craft movements and cases entirely in-house. From micro-sandblasting to hand beveling, every surface is finished by artisans.

Innovation

Patented Magic Gold, translucent sapphire and high-tech ceramic fuse tradition with science. We push beyond steel to create pieces that feel architectural on the wrist.

Legacy

For four decades we have partnered with artists, athletes and avant-garde thinkers to tell stories through limited editions that become family heirlooms.
